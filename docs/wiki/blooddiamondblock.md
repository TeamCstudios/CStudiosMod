# Blood Diamond Block

The Blood Diamond Block is a way to compact your amount of Blood Diamond Gems.

## Crafting

1) You can put 9 Blood Diamond Gems into one block.

- Master Sword
- Blood Diamond Apple
- Blood Diamond (Gem)
